II. WHAT TYPES OF INFORMATION ARE COLLECTED AND WHY?

Welltek may collect personally identifiable information or non-personally identifiable information through your use of the DARWIN app. Personally identifiable information (or personal information) is information that personally identifies you, such as your name, postal address and email address, and non-personally identifiable information is information that does not personally identify you, including anonymous information and aggregated data about users of the DARWIN app. Information is collected in order to provide services to you, tailor services to you based on your preferences and display information to you about the environment inside your home, as well as to improve our services and offerings and for other general research purposes. Information can be collected if you submit it to us or it may be collected automatically through technology. The DARWIN app uses information it collects in order to recognize and identify the property, sensors and other appliances and products in your home, and collect data generated from those sensors and other appliances and products. We also may use your personal information to respond to your requests and provide support, to communicate with you, to provide updates to you about relevant products and services, and otherwise provide our products and services to you.

a. Information submitted by you

You may submit information to us when you use the DARWIN app. For instance, you may submit information in order to authenticate your credentials and input your preferences including your name, email address, social connection (single sign on user option), log-in and password credentials created by you, additional users that may access the DARWIN app, your preferences, your favorites, your schedule, and other information you provide to help us customize our services for you and optimize system performance.

b. Location Information

You may submit location information, including your time zone and address, in your preferences and certain location information may be collected automatically by using the DARWIN app such as Internet Protocol address, street address (including city, state, territory and postcode), and the products and appliances in your home compatible with the DARWIN app. Time zone and location data is used to customize settings in your home, such as triggering dawn simulation and controlling other lighting timers. By registering and using the DARWIN app, you consent to such collection and use of this information.

c. Device and environmental information collected automatically

The DARWIN app recognizes devices in your home that you choose to connect to the DARWIN app and collects information about those devices to display the information to you so that you can better understand the environment inside your home. Whenever you select to activate devices via the DARWIN app, we automatically receive and record information on our servers, logs and databases from the DARWIN app or device(s). In particular, the DARWIN app is designed to allow you to connect various devices you choose to connect in your home (for example your lighting, HVAC system, and/or water filter) to the DARWIN app. While connected to the DARWIN app, these devices automatically report information to our servers, logs and databases (including information you may have provided when setting up the devices or the DARWIN app). For instance, if you connect an indoor environmental quality (“IEQ”) sensor to the DARWIN app and activate the IEQ portion of the DARWIN app, the IEQ data (along with other information such as applicable room, device name and location) will be transmitted and displayed to you via the DARWIN app. Specifically, your home contains sensors to help you monitor the environment in your home. Some information the DARWIN app collects from your sensors and displays for you on the DARWIN app if you activate this feature include temperature, relative humidity and air quality conditions, such as Particulate Matter 10, Particulate Matter 2.5, carbon dioxide, total volatile organic compounds, temperature and humidity.

d. Use of Non-personal information and aggregated data

The DARWIN app collects data about your use of the DARWIN app such as commands triggered and other DARWIN app usage metrics. Welltek may use non-personally identifiable information and anonymized, aggregated data to understand better how the DARWIN app is used, including average time spent using the DARWIN app and pages viewed, to provide users with customized services and information, and to gather other relevant statistics and information. Welltek collects this information to measure the use of the DARWIN app and to improve the content of the DARWIN app. Such information, and other information we derive from an individual user’s information, supports our continued research, and allows us to continue to grow and improve our services and products.

e. Information collected by technology

Welltek and its agents use technologies like cookies to help you use our services more easily and help us continuously improve the DARWIN app and our services. Personal and non-personal information that may be collected using such technologies may include IP addresses, time spent on the DARWIN app, location information and a user’s internet service provider. A “cookie” is a small data file that a website can send to your browser, which may then be stored on your hard drive to help the website “remember” information about you when you return to the website, such as your preferences for the website or account identifications. We use simple session based cookies for logged in users on the website linked to the DARWIN app to track active sessions. You may have the option of setting your browser to reject cookies; however, doing so may affect the performance of the DARWIN app or restrict the way in which we may provide our services to you.
